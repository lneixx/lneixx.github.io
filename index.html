About Me

Turning data into decisions,
and AI into something safer.

A quick snapshot of who I am, what drives me, and why I care about building intelligent systems responsibly.

I'm a BSIT – Business Analytics graduate with a strong foundation in data analytics, predictive modeling, and machine learning. I don't just analyze data — I build systems that help people make smarter decisions.

During my internship at FPD Asia Property Services Inc, I worked on Davies, an AI-powered chatbot, where I designed system prompts, tested for prompt injection and jailbreak vulnerabilities, and helped improve the bot's routing logic and response reliability.

That experience sharpened my understanding of AI security and prompt engineering — skills that are increasingly critical as organizations deploy large language models. I'm passionate about making AI systems not just functional, but trustworthy.

On the analytics side, I've built classification models that predict student drop risk and crime case resolution likelihood — projects that blend technical depth with real social impact.

I graduated from Bulacan State University and I'm currently part of Techno Paragons League (Feb 2025 – 2026), where I continue developing my analytics, visualization, and collaboration skills through applied projects.
